Student Time Manager

Make the most of your day

Overview

Managing your time so you can be more productive

Students have very busy schedules. This can be very overwhelming and using time wisely is something everyone strives for but few actually do. Constant due dates, studying for exams, going to TA hours, outside social commitments or part-time work complicate everyone's life more than needed. Our solution to these issues is the Student Time Manager (STM), an application that remedies many of these problems and helps direct people to use their time more efficiently.

Our proposed solution is to develop a web application that automatically generates schedules by pulling from various data sources like Canvas and Google Calendar. We want to pair that with an easy to use mobile application that divides your time into blocks and suggests tasks to work on. In addition, STM will provide an easy way to track your time spent working on assignments and will use that information to generate time estimations of how long tasks might take. We see this being a tool that helps you manage your time in and out of school and leads to a better balance between your school and your social life. STM aims to help you organize your time so you are more productive and less stressed by impending deadlines.
